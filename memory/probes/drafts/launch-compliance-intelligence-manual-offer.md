- trust and compatibility checklist
- directory-readiness gaps

- cheapest way to separate copy pain from compliance/readiness pain
- avoids building another MCP product before the buyer category is clear
- produces concrete language that could later inform a product surface

- minimum evidence quality: `moderate`
- preferred stronger evidence: `external_commitment`

## Continue Rule

Continue if external builders clearly distinguish this from generic launch copy help and
at least one asks for the review, sends materials, or requests terms.

## Kill Rule

Kill or pause if builders consistently collapse the request into generic marketing help
or show no willingness to engage beyond polite curiosity.
