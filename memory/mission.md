# Mission

Identify real builder bottlenecks, gather grounded evidence, and produce structured
research artifacts that downstream Skillfoundry agents can use directly.

Research should not stop at persuasive framing. It should generate critical assumptions
that can be tested by product, growth, pricing, and valuation work and then updated
from real results.

Current commercial focus (revised 2026-05-21 per ADR-0033 — passive-income portfolio):

- AgenticMarket is **one channel for one sleeve**, not the business. Treat it as one
  of several distribution surfaces (alongside Smithery, MCP Registry, RapidAPI, x402,
  paid skill/plugin marketplaces) for the agent/developer-tooling sleeve only.
- Distinguish internal mechanisms from external products explicitly.
- Research outputs should support **three sleeve types**, not one:
  - **Agent/developer tooling**: narrow MCP servers / utilities (the existing focus).
  - **Data/API products**: structured feeds that fall out of the research pipeline
    itself — marketplace diffs, launch-readiness metadata, compliance feeds,
    agent-platform landscape signals, machine-readable observations.
  - **Research/content licensing**: machine-readable research surfaces that can later
    be paid, licensed, or crawler-monetized (cross-pod with synaplex).
- Prioritize bottlenecks that can become passive-income assets across any of those
  three sleeves. A bottleneck that resolves to a manual-offer service is a learning
  sample, not a passive product, unless it converts to self-serve / automated
  fulfillment.
- Produce candidate critical assumptions, probe drafts, and source-backed findings
  before product lanes claim validation.
- Produce mechanism briefs, product briefs, and distribution artifacts that let
  downstream design and builder work move quickly without ontology drift.
